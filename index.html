Lyft Driver Retention Strategy

Ran survival analysis on 193,000+ rides to find the earliest disengagement signals, tested whether a simple risk model held up against a complex one, and designed a targeted retention experiment around the drivers most likely to leave.

Used Car Pricing Benchmark

Pricing 97,673 UK used-car listings by feel leaves money on the table in both directions. Built a transparent benchmark that cut typical pricing error from 9.61% to 7.77%, and just as usefully, flagged which vehicles the model shouldn't be trusted on, routing those to human review.

NYC Short-Term Rental Regulatory Exposure

New York's Local Law 18 requires short-term rentals to register, but enforcement depends on seeing who hasn't. Built a nine-month dashboard from 325,052 listing snapshots showing 59.5% of April 2026 short-stay supply sat outside registration, with exemption exposure concentrated in a small group of professional host accounts.
