NYC Short-Term Rental Regulatory Exposure

New York's Local Law 18 requires short-term rentals to register, but enforcement depends on seeing who hasn't. Built a nine-month dashboard from 325,052 listing snapshots showing 59.5% of April 2026 short-stay supply sat outside registration, with exemption exposure concentrated in a small group of professional host accounts.
