Selected work

Flagship case study

Estimating Driver Lifetime Value

Using the public Lyft Data Challenge dataset (~937 drivers, ~194k rides), I estimated a driver's lifetime value at ~$1.7–1.9K in net revenue and showed that a driver's first seven days predict both their value and their churn — reframing retention spend from "rescue the strugglers" to "grow the engaged middle." Validated the lifetime with a survival model and built an early-churn classifier to pressure-test the findings.
